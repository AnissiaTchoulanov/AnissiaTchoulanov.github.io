INTERNSHIP REPORT


After one month internship in the digital and fashion agency Mezo, I wrote an account of said internship, to present all the projects I had worked on. I chose to reference the aesthetic of press reviews, so I worked on a newspaper in print and used monochrome colors.

Photography is one of the main activities of this agency, so I chose to include pictures to illustrate the text.

This was a creative work about the graphic and visual identity of Mezo. I contributed to the production of several posters and animations, to a graphic research for the interface of the web-site: creation of a loading page, of a favicon and some gifs.
